Hi, I’m Nicole!

I’m a web and graphic designer at the Nebraska Department of Agriculture. Officially, I’m responsible for developing, designing, and implementing department-wide communications and promotional materials. Which really means… I work every day to share the story of Nebraska agriculture, on a local, national and global scale. Let’s take a look at some of my work!

I regularly work on graphic design projects at the department, but am particularly proud of the NDA Feature and our Commodity and Trade Profiles. They have been heavily used by our department and we frequently receive compliments on how informative and well-designed they are:

  • The NDA Feature tells the stories of the people, products and programs that contribute to the success of Nebraska's agriculture industry. My coworker and I work collaboratively to develop these quarterly stories.
  • The Commodity and Trade Profiles highlight statewide production and exports and how trade ultimately brings value to every county in the state. These profiles are utilized on international trade missions and with international trade groups. Additionally, these profiles are used by Nebraska commodity boards, congressmen and senators, and by the Governor's Office.

Additionally, I am responsible for the creation of several e-newsletters at the department. These e-newsletters are created collaboratively with coworkers, with my primary focus on design. We utilize MailChimp for these e-newsletters.

  • The Nebraska Ag Update is a weekly e-newsletter that I designed and am responsible for sending to our 560+ subscribers on Fridays.
  • The Plant Health Protection Update is a e-newsletter that is periodically sent out, usually quarterly. I designed this newsletter and work with one our ag program specialists for the newsletter content.
