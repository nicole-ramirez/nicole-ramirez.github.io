Videos

As a child, I developed a passion for producing videos. My sisters and I would produce our own “newscasts” and would research topics, film and edit video. It's been a while since I was making "newscasts" in my parents' basement with my sisters, but the process behind creating videos remains the same: research, film and edit.

NDA Employees

A video series highlighting the work of Nebraska Department of Agriculture (NDA) employees.

 
On-Farm Readiness Reviews with Caitlyn Andrews

If you're looking for guidance on how to best prepare your farm for Food Safety Modernization Act Produce Safety Rule inspections, then an On-Farm Readiness Review might be best for you.

To learn more about the Nebraska Department of Agriculture's Produce Safety Program, visit www.nda.nebraska.gov/producesafety

Feed Inspections with Chelsea Mills

Did you know that NDA's Commercial Feed Program inspects feed manufacturers, transporters, and distributors/retailers? Inspectors sample feed to assure compliance with state and federal feed safety and labeling regulations. Agriculture Inspection Specialist Chelsea Mills takes us step-by-step through the feed inspection process.

Pump and Meter Inspections with Mike Johnson

Did you know that the Nebraska Department of Agriculture is working for you at the fuel pump? NDA's Weights and Measures staff work to ensure the accuracy of commercial weighing and measuring devices. See how inspector Mike Johnson makes sure you're getting what you pay for at the pump.

Emerald Ash Borer with Julie Van Meter

Did you know that emerald ash borers can be spread through the movement of infested ash nursery stock, ash firewood and other ash material? Entomology Program Manager Julie Van Meter shares more about how NDA inspectors are conducting nursery stock inspections and checking for insect and disease problems.

Standards Lab with Joel Lavicky

Did you know that the Nebraska Department of Agriculture (NDA) provides calibration services for service personnel, industry and weights and measures inspectors? Metrologist Joel Lavicky tells us about NDA's Standards Laboratory and how NDA is working to protect consumers.

Christmas Tree Inspections with Libby Smith

Every holiday season, the Nebraska Department of Agriculture inspects live cut Christmas trees before they are purchased and brought home. NDA Inspector Libby Smith shows us what she’s looking for during a typical Christmas tree inspection.

Large Scale Inspections with Kurt Wenninghoff

Did you know the Nebraska Department of Agriculture inspects large scales? Inspector Kurt Wenninghoff tells us about these and why they are important.

Refined Fuel and LP Gas Inspections with Mike Boehler

Did you know the Nebraska Department of Agriculture inspects refined fuel and liquefied petroleum gas trucks? Inspector Mike Boehler tells us about these inspections and why they are important.

Animal Health with Dr. Jim Stuart

Dr. Jim Stuart, a veterinary field officer with the Nebraska Department of Agriculture (NDA), shares what NDA is doing to protect animal health. Learn more about salmonella pullorum and avian influenza testing.

Festival Food Inspections

Paul Del Signore, a food inspector with the Nebraska Department of Agriculture, shares the differences between festival and restaurant inspections.

NDA Food Inspections

Melanie Kunc, a food inspector with the Nebraska Department of Agriculture, shares more about what NDA is doing to keep consumers safe.
